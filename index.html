Who We Are

Bangla Band Parallel Universe (BBPU) is a passionate social marketing group laser-focused on the vibrant Bengali rock music scene. We bridge the gap between talented bands and their audience through compelling video storytelling and strategic online promotion across Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube.

Our mission is to give Bangla rock the platform it deserves, helping bands grow their reach and connect with fans worldwide.
